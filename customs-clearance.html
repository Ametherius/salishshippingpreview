Customs Clearance

Salish Shipping Co is proud to offer its clients exclusive rates with a Manitoba-based customs broker to help reduce costs associated with importing products from foreign countries. Experience has shown us that most Canadian small businesses simply pay whatever their courier charges to clear their shipments. On $1,000 worth of imports, this is more than $120 through UPS. Our partner charges about half of what the couriers charge, and they're a small, family-owned business, just like us.

Please get in touch to see how their rates compare.

We must all work together towards reconciliation for a better Canada.

The Salish Sea has been an integral part of my life for years and will continue to be forever. My home, Salt Spring Island, is ancestral and unceded traditional territory of the Hul’q’umi’num and SENĆOŦEN speaking peoples. Most land where we ship to and ship from is also unceded traditional territory of various indigenous peoples. By recognizing these realities, and sharing this recognition, my goal is to give respect where respect is due.

-Gabriel McPhee
